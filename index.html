WHO ARE WE ?
Starting out as IEEE NITC SB's event to promote start up ideas, Interface has now grown into the largest summit in the Malabar region exclusive to budding start-ups. Spanning over 3 days, it provides a platform for student entrepreneurs to discuss their ideas and products with the experienced ones. The primary objective of Interface is to find and support the best and most innovative startup ideas from the numerous abstracts and submissions by colleges and incubation cells across the State.

Varun Chandran

Mr. Varun Chandran is the Founder of Corporate 360 Pte Ltd., a firm which has operations in four continents today, and serves as its Chief Executive Officer. Mr. Chandran is the leader in B2B sales intelligence. He is responsible for advancing C360's vision to deliver best-in-class marketing data and world class sales & marketing analytics software to global markets. He holds executive leadership honors from HaaS School of Business, University of California, Berkeley.

Afsal Salu

Mr. Afsal Salu is the co-founder of Delyver.com, India's first hyperlocal on-demand marketplace in 2010, much before the words 'hyperlocal' and 'marketplace' became common parlance in Indian startup community. Delyver got acquired by BigBasket.com, India's largest e-grocer, in 2015. Post acquisition, Mr. Salu built the Express Delivery business for BigBasket from scratch to 2nd largest BU within 7 months. After a successful stint in Retail, he is now onto his 2nd venture – BestDoc, an intelligent Patient Relationship Management (PRM) for Hospitals.

Vinay J Kynadi

President & CEO, Herbz International is an experienced sales & marketing professional. Mr. Kynadi has launched and promoted natural product brands like Invigo, Vivacy and Aryoga after his tenure with the Coca-Cola Company where he managed some of the world's most valuable brands.

Praveen Parameswar

An emerging Organization Development (OD) Strategist, lifologist, and researcher in ‘Human Behaviour’, is the CEO of Thoughts Academy, a rapidly growing company in the field of Organization Development Interventions. He helps various MNCs, NGOs and massive Government projects to multiply growth through capitalizing the full potential of their human resource. He is also a consultant in education planning and management for a handful of Higher Education Institutions across India and abroad. He has done presentations in more than 1000 platforms across India and delivered session in more than 6 countries including the UK and the UAE, and has been a panellist for national television channels such as Times-Now and NDTV. He currently hosts ‘Samvadam’, a celebrated Socio-Political talk show, for Doordarsan, Kerala.

NURTURING PROGRAM

♦ Product Prototyping ♦
10 teams are chosen for Product Prototyping from the received applications.
Mentors are assigned from our network of incubators and other collaborating organizations for the selected teams.
Technical Support and access to NITC, FABLAB, and other facilities are given to the selected team members.
Each team will receive a grant of max. Rs.5,000/- via the assigned mentor.
The teams are monitored with weekly report submission.
They also get a Direct Entry to Interface ’18

♦ Idea Pitching ♦
10 teams chosen for Idea Pitching from the received applications.
Mentors are assigned from our network of incubators and other collaborating organizations for the selected teams.
The teams are monitored with weekly report submission.
They also get a Direct Entry to Interface ’18

♦ Startup Pitching ♦
All promising Start-ups applied for Interface ’18 are chosen for Acceleration.
Mentors are assigned from our network of incubators and other collaborating organizations for the selected teams.
The teams are monitored with weekly report submission.
They also get a Direct Entry to Interface ’18

Guidelines

- Application form to be filled before 1st August, 2018 with the abstracts.
- Product description must brief it’s unique features and technical aspects.
- Once shortlisted, the participants will be contacted via mail.
- Participants are expected to have a Business Plan/prototype by the end of the mentoring.
- Ideas/Products must be original. Plagiarism will not be tolerated.

Abstract

- Name of the product/idea and the names of the team members with their email ids.
- A description of the project in less than 200 words.
